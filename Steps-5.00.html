How do you share within Prod Environment?

From the Services account, you can choose to share a report or dashboard with anyone using a public link. For privacy reasons, you are required to have permission to generate public link.
Click on File > Publish to Web > (agree to the fine print) and two links will be generated.


1.You can share your report via email using the link
2.You can use the HTML link to embed your report in a blog or website


After setting up both Dev and Prod Environments, it will be possible to share dashboards and report such as this. Sharing with co-workers and setting up security measures will be much more efficient. Additional permissions such as who can edit and who can only view is also available within Power BI Server. These dashboards and reports can also be viewed through mobile devices by employees making everything all the more convenient and accessible.
