How to Setup the Prod (Cloud) environment?

Setting up the Prod Environment
You can access the Prod Environment using the same credentials provided by the business to publish your report
Go to: app.powerbi.com

Login with your email and password

ex) Username: CecilaRobbins@CompanyEnterprise.com


Power BI Services
Once you are logged in, you can access your report on the Power BI Services Account
of the business. In this case it would be Company Enterprise

1. Click on My Workspace. You will get the drop-down menu where you can access your:
a. Dashboard (if you have created one)
b. Reports that you have published
c. Workbooks
d. Datasets backing up your reports.


2. You can interact with your published reports and work with them just like you could in Power BI Desktop.
3. You also could create Dashboards by pinning individual visualization tiles or entire report page to the Dashboard.

4. You can also edit uploaded reports within Power BI Server.
