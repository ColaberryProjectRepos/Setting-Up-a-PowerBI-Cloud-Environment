What are the Power BI Environments?

First, let us go over what exactly an Environment is for a business and the different types. There are two types of working environments with Power BI:

1. Developer (Dev) Environment
This is the Power BI Desktop installed on your computer where, as a developer, you will be building all your Power BI reports.

2. Production (Prod) Environment
This is the Power BI Services account where all completed reports and work can be published on where others can have access to it.

To first set up the Cloud environment, you first need to set up the dev environment.
