How to create a dev report for the cloud environment?

Publishing the Completed Report


When you are done with creating the report, the next step is to publish it to the Production Environment so others can have access to it.

1. You can publish your report right from the Power BI Desktop Ribbon.


Click on the Publish button on the Home Tab


2. Alternatively, you can go to the File menu and select Publish > Publish to Power BI


 To be able to publish your report, you need access to a Power BI Services Account provided by the business.
When you click on Publish, you will be asked to log in.

Usually the username will be formatted as such:

[FullName]@[CompanyName].com

Here is an example log in:

Username: CecilaRobbins@CompanyEnterprise.com

After logging into the account provided by the business through Power BI Desktop, a menu will show up asking where it is the report is to be published on.

Select ‘My workspace’ to publish your work.
